PixoPet, Your Pixel Pet

Choose a pet. Build its home. Watch it grow.

PixoPet is a pixel-style virtual pet game where you can choose your own home pet, take care of it every day, decorate its home, and help it grow through fun activities. Feed, play, and customize your pet while enjoying a relaxing retro pixel experience designed for casual players and pet lovers of all ages.

About PixoPet

Classic comfort, modern play

PixoPet is a relaxing and fun virtual pet game inspired by classic pet games. Players can adopt and care for a variety of adorable pets, each with their own personalities and traits.

Grow, decorate, discover

Feed, play with, and nurture your pets while decorating their cozy rooms with furniture, wallpapers, and fun accessories. As your pets grow, you unlock new activities, rewards, and items—including mini-games, seasonal events, and hidden surprises.

Designed for all ages, PixoPet encourages creativity, responsibility, and daily interaction, offering endless enjoyment for casual gamers and pet lovers alike.

Highlights Included:

  • Variety of pets: A variety of unique pets, each with their own personalities and needs.
  • Caring & interaction: Feed, play, and bond with your pets daily to help them develop new skills.
  • Room customization: Build and decorate a room that reflects your creativity.
  • Mini-games and fun activities: Play mini-games, join seasonal events, and discover hidden rewards.
  • Growth and rewards: Unlock surprises, earn items, and watch your pets grow and develop.
